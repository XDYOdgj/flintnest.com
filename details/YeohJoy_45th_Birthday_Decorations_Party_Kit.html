Celebrate Your 45th Birthday with Style & Nostalgia

Create a memorable and elegant birthday celebration with our complete 45th birthday decoration kit. Perfect for any occasion, this set brings a retro-inspired feel to your party, making it both festive and unforgettable.

Our kit includes 16 high-quality vintage-themed items, such as a hallmark 45th birthday backdrop, a bold Black Gold banner, and vintage anniversary cards. Each piece is designed to add charm and a nostalgic touch, ideal for creating a warm and lively atmosphere for your special day.

The Black Gold banner measures 59x35 inches and features copper loops for easy hanging, ensuring it becomes the standout centerpiece of your party. Paired with 7pcs Hanging Swirl and 6pcs 10 inch paper poms, this set offers a variety of decorative options to enhance your event and create stunning photo backdrops.

These decorations are versatile and suitable for use indoors or outdoors. Made from durable, washable, and tear-resistant polyester materials, they are built to last and can be reused for multiple occasions. Whether you're celebrating a 45th birthday for a father, husband, or friend, this kit is an ideal choice for any celebration.
