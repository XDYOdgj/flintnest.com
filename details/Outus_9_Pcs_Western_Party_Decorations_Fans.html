Western Party Fans Set - 9 Pieces for Rustic & Vibrant Decor

Handcrafted for Lasting Impact

These western party fans are designed to bring a rustic, vibrant character to your next cowboy-themed celebration. Their bold red and blue bandana patterns make them a standout addition to any decor setup.

Versatile Sizes for Dynamic Displays

With three sizes included (8", 12", 16"), you can create a visually striking display that adds dimension and depth to your party. These fans are easy to assemble using the included lines or stickers and can be hung from ceilings or walls for a simple, effective setup. Whether you're hosting an indoor gathering or an outdoor bash, these fans are the perfect choice.

3 Sizes Included

Choose from 8", 12", and 16" fans to suit your event's needs. The variety of sizes allows for creative arrangement and visual balance.

Perfect for Western Events
