Creative Rainbow Wooden Loose Parts Set for Kids

This beautiful wooden loose parts set brings endless creative possibilities to your child's playtime. With 7 unique shapes like mushrooms, honeycombs, and more, it transforms simple play into a rich, imaginative experience. Colorful and versatile, this set helps kids develop early learning skills such as color recognition, pattern matching, and visual stimulation.

Made from high-quality, child-safe materials like New Zealand pine, European beech, and subtropical lotus, these wooden pieces are non-toxic and durable.
